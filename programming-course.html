Programming and Operative Systems Course

Have you ever wanted to learn how to program but don't know where to start? This is the perfect course for you! In 9 online sessions, you will learn the fundamental concepts of programming.

The course consists of 9 stages, a total of 28 hours. This course is delivered via live video calls on Discord, and it is 100% online. The languages covered in the course are Java and Python, and you only need internet and a computer to program.

Stages

  • Stage 1. Train your logic to program. (1 hour).
  • Stage 2. Flowcharts. (1 hour).
  • Stage 3. Preparing workspace. (1 - 2 hours).
  • Stage 4. Basic and intermediate algorithms in Java. (3 - 5 hours).
  • Stage 5. Basic and intermediate algorithms in Python. (3 - 5 hours).

  • Stage 7. Introduction to virtual machines. (4 hours).
  • Stage 8. Introduction to Linux (Ubuntu) and command terminal. (2 hours).

Personal Finance Course

Are you in red numbers or want to improve your finances to pay off debt? I'll teach you how to manage your money the best way possible! This course will only take you 6 online sessions and you'll be able to properly manage your finances.

The course consists of 6 stages, a total of 12 hours. The course is delivered through live video calls on Discord and is 100% online. The course is more practical than theoretical, as I provide a Google Sheets template for you to enter your financial data and easily manage your money. Don't worry if you don't know how to use Google Sheets, I will teach you what you need to know. You only need internet access and preferably a computer.

Stages

  • Stage 1. Theory of fundamental concepts to understand the context of the course. (1 hour).
  • Stage 2. How to make a financial decision correctly?. (1 hour).
  • Stage 3. How to use a credit card and know which one suits you best?. (1 -2 hours).
  • Stage 4. How to make a financial plan and projection of income and expenses?. (4 hours).
  • Stage 5. What are the Months Without Interest?. (2 hours).
  • Stage 6. What to do with debt?. (2 hours).
